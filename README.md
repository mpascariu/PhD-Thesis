# PhD-Thesis

## Title: Modelling and forecasting mortality
## Author: Marius D. Pascariu
## Defence date: 25 October, 2018

This thesis has been developed during my appointment as Ph.D. Research Fellow at the Institute of Public Health within the [University of Southern Denmark](https://www.sdu.dk/en/) and during my research stay at the [Australian National University](http://demography.cass.anu.edu.au), from March 2015 to October 2018. The work was conducted within the \emph{Modelling and forecasting age-specific death at older ages} project [No.95--103--31186], with the financial support of the [SCOR Corporate Foundation for Science](https://www.scor.com/en/scor-corporate-foundation-science).
